The Rodriquezes

Pete & Yvonne Rodriquez, mother of Jeannette Rodriquez and grandparents to Mark Waid and Ethan Moore, have been happily married for 50 years. In other words, their marriage is what's called an old fogey union. They met in 1967 in Albuquerque, NM. Pete had a very nice GTO, and he decided to go to the A&W Rootbeer to eat and join the cruise circuit. Pete and Yvonne saw eachother while cruising on Central and waved at each other, so they both pulled over at a Lerners parking lot to talk. This seemingly simple encounter is what turned into this beautiful 50 year communion.
