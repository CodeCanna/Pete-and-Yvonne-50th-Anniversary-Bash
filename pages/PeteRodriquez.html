The Rodriquezes

Pete Rodriquez was born June 29th, 1946 in Albuquerque, New Mexico to a poor family. His big journey started when he was drafted into the Army at age 18 to fight in the Vietnam War. He answered his country's call and served for two years before finishing his duty and retiring from the military. However, fitting back into civilian life after two years of violence and bloodshed was when the real hardship truly began. He met Yvonne when he was still recovering from his trauma from the military, and she has helped him to fit back into society and keep him sane ever since.
