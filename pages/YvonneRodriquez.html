The Rodriquezes

Yvonne Rodriquez, formerly Helling, was born November 27, 1945 in Albuquerque, New Mexico to a beautiful family. She attended St. Mary's Catholic School. She started her 43 year long career at APS in 1966 after graduating from TVI's first Secretarial class. She was Head of the Secretarial Union, and later was promoted to President. Yvonne retired from APS in 2009, but stayed in the Secretarial Union until 2016. She continued to serve as an advisor to the President of the SU until late 2019.
